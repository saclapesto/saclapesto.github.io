About Us

History

In 2023, we started InterFrames, a company aimed at changing the animation industry with new software that uses algorithms for interpolation. As animators and software developers, we saw a big gap in the market: the current tools often held us back, making it hard to control smooth movements in our animations. We wanted to fix that.Our goal was to create a simple platform that lets animators customize their animations easily. With our advanced algorithms, InterFrames allows for smooth transitions between keyframes, helping us get the effects we want without hassle.We aim to make this software a popular tool in leading studios around the world. By working with major companies, we hope to set a new standard in animation technology, giving animators more creative control and transforming how animated stories are told.
